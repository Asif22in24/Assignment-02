Change Your Habits

Changing habits involves setting clear goals, breaking down steps, and staying consistent. Replace old habits with positive ones, reward progress, and be patient with setbacks. Over time, persistence leads to lasting change.

BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool that estimates body fat based on weight and height. It helps determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. While it's a quick health indicator it doesn't measure body composition or differentiate between muscle and fat.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 - and Above Obese

Calculate your BMI

Calculate Your BMI is a quick tool that helps you determine your Body Mass Index by entering your

Healthy Diet

A healthy diet focuses on whole grains, lean proteins, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats, while limiting processed foods, sugars, and salt. It supports overall well-being and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.

Fitness

Fitness is the state of physical health achieved through exercise, nutrition, and rest.
It enhances physical abilities, mental health, and energy while reducing disease risk, promoting a healthier lifestyle.
